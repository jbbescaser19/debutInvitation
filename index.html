About the Debutant

"She blooms with grace, shines with purpose, and walks forward with a heart full of love."

Sophia Khate R. Arganda turns eighteen this December — and what better way to celebrate this milestone than surrounded by the people who have shaped her story. Graceful, kind, and full of wonder, Sofia has grown into a young woman whose warmth lights up every room she enters.

This evening is a celebration not just of her birthday, but of every chapter, every lesson, and every beautiful moment that has brought her to this night. We are so honored to have you witness the beginning of her next great adventure.
